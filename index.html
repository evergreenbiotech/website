About Us

Established in 2019, Evergreen Biotechnology Singapore Pte. Ltd. is a leading importer and wholesaler of premium traditional Chinese medicine (TCM) products, dedicated to providing high-quality, authentic ingredients to businesses worldwide.

关于我们
